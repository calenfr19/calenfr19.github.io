About Me

I am a fourth year PhD candidate studying Atmospheric Science at the Univeristy of California, Davis. My research focuses on climate extremes, specifically heatwaves.

Research

My research is split into three main topics, statistical evaluation of heatwaves across climate products, evaluating heatwave statistics across various oceanic modes of variability, and quantifying heatwave drivers.

Prior to UC Davis I worked on wildfires in western Washington and Oregon and studied the relationship between northeast Pacific SSTs and Pacific Northwest temperatures.
